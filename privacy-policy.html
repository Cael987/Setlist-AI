Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1/4/25

1. Information We Collect

Setlist AI collects the following types of information:

  • Audio recordings (temporary, for song identification only)
  • Video data (when using the video analysis feature)
  • Playlist information and liked songs
  • Music service authentication tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Identify songs using Shazam's technology
  • Create and manage your playlists
  • Connect with Apple Music and Spotify services
  • Improve our song recognition accuracy

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services:

  • Shazam for song recognition
  • Apple Music for playlist export and playback
  • Spotify for playlist export and playback

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device. Audio recordings are temporary and are only used for immediate song recognition. We do not store or transmit audio recordings to our servers.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored playlists and liked songs
  • Delete your data from the app
  • Opt out of specific features
